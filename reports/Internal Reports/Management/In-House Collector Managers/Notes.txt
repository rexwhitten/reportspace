Days Old Graph - This report displays the file inventory (by collector) in graphical format to reveal who may have files that appear neglected in the eyes of a client accessing the dashboard.

Days Old Subreport - only accessible when clicking through the Days Old Graph report.

Overdue Diary - This report graphically displays the queues (list of work to be done) by collector to reveal unworked tasks.

Overdue Diary subreport - only accessibly when clicking through the Overdue Diary report.
